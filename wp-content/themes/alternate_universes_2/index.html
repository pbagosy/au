MTG GPT for Grand Prix Philadelphia at AU,
Wilmington, Sunday March 30th

Magic: The Gathering | Speical Events | Wilmington Store

Alternate Universes, Wilmington will be hosting a Grand Prix Trial for Grand Prix, Philadelphia on Sunday, March 30th. Registration opens at 11AM and play will begin at noon. Format is Sealed.

Prizes: 4 booster packs for each participating will put in the prize pool!

Grand Prix Trials (GPTs) are smaller, regional Magic Tournaments that award at least one 2-round bye to the appropriate Grand Prix.
